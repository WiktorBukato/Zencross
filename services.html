SERVICE DETAILS

What each starting point is designed to clarify

Primary path

Feasibility Assessment

A structured assessment for teams that already have a deployment target and need confidence before committing engineering budget.

  • Validate system architecture and stack dependencies
  • Identify module, eSIM, connectivity, cloud, and delivery roles
  • Define a practical next-step roadmap before pilot or scale

Entry path

Discovery Call

A lighter first conversation for early-stage projects where the use case, geography, or requirements are still being shaped.

  • Clarify use-case maturity and deployment assumptions
  • Identify whether feasibility, consultation, or further discovery fits
  • Surface obvious technical or ecosystem constraints early

Consultation

  • Compare stack options against deployment constraints
  • Resolve focused questions before broader feasibility work
