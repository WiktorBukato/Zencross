Privacy Policy

This policy explains how ZENCROSS™ Alliance handles website, inquiry, and feasibility request information.

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Scope Information Use Sharing Choices Contact

Scope

This policy applies to information submitted through this website, including contact requests, feasibility assessment requests, and related business communications.

Information we collect

We may collect contact details, company information, project context, target geography, expected deployment scale, use-case information, and any other details you choose to share.

Like most websites, basic technical information may also be processed, such as browser type, device information, referring pages, and general usage data.

How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, evaluate project fit, prepare feasibility conversations, route requests to the right ecosystem role, improve website content, and maintain business records.

Sharing with alliance participants

When needed to respond to a deployment request, information may be shared with relevant ZENCROSS Alliance participants or service providers who support evaluation, connectivity, cloud, product development, or delivery work.

We do not sell inquiry information. We may disclose information when required by law, to protect rights and security, or in connection with ordinary business operations.

Cookies and third-party services

The website may use cookies or similar technologies for essential functionality, performance measurement, or analytics. Some embedded services, such as fonts or external links, may be provided by third parties under their own policies.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of business contact information where applicable. We may need to retain limited records where required for legal, security, or operational reasons.

Security and retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required or permitted.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at hello@zencross-alliance.com.
